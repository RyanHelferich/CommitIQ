What To Include

Tell us who has this problem. Be specific.
Tell us what they do today without your app, and why that is painful.
If you built this for yourself or your team, explain who else would need it too.
Show how your app makes the user's life better.
Give us a working demo link we can open.
Give us enough code and demo instructions to verify what you built.
Scoring Breakdown

Demand Reality & Problem Severity (25%): Judges look for a real, specific problem with clear pain, not a vague idea.
Target Customer & Market Scope (20%): Judges look for a clear user and evidence the problem exists beyond one isolated case.
Solution Fit & Product Design (20%): Judges look for a product that makes the user's life obviously better.
Technical Execution & Demo Proof (20%): Judges look for working software they can open, verify, and understand.
Differentiation & Investment Readiness (15%): Judges look for why this could become important, durable, or meaningfully different.
